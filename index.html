About Us

M/s Shivam Constructions is a proprietorship construction firm established in 2014, owned and managed by Mr. Balkishan Sharma. The firm specializes in road construction and civil infrastructure works, with a strong focus on quality execution and timely project delivery.

Under the leadership of Mr. Balkishan Sharma, the firm has successfully executed multiple government projects across Mathura, Vrindavan, Hathras (Uttar Pradesh), and Ashok Nagar (Madhya Pradesh). The firm has built a reputation for reliable workmanship and compliance with approved technical specifications.

We have worked with various government bodies including the Public Works Department (PWD), Nagar Nigam Mathura–Vrindavan, and Jal Kal Vibhag. Our work focuses on delivering durable, safe, and high-quality infrastructure while strictly following approved specifications and timelines.
